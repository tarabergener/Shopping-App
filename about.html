About Holly

A homemaker in every sense of the word, Holly (RecipeFinder CEO), like many other mohters

struggled with finding new recipes that were delicious and simple to make.

We have all been there right? You get home from work at 5:00 pm, you look in

the pantry and find nothing! Well, not nothing... a slew of ingredients you

don't know what to do with. You're hungry, the kids are hungry, so what now?

You eat out... again. The homemaker in you wants to spend all evening creating

that piece of art that appeals to all the senses; it smells like heaven, and looks

like it just popped off the cover of Taste of Home magazine! But instead you feel

bad about being tired. You don't want to get back into the car to shop for extra

ingredients and you don't know what to do with what you have...

Well, look no further! This app was specifically designed for moms like Holly.

Don't worry about the hassle of not getting your mobile shopping order done until

tomorrow. Use what you have to make a delicious meal tonight!
